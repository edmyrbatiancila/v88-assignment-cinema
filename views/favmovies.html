My Favourite Movies

This is based on my own opinion, feel free to add your own favorite movie here.

Extraction 2020

Is an american action film, which is about rescuing the boy. This movie is so engaging to watch. I love how they introduce indian culture in this film and the action cinematics are so breath-taking. I enjoy every fight scenes.

Perfume: The Story of a Murderer 2006

Mind blown movie for me, it's about a man who is obsess of love and affection that he did not get from his childhood, unintentionally knowing that what he did is an act of a hideous crimes that forever marks in the history of mankind. A movie worth to watch.

Avatar: The Way of Water 2022

I love the animation of this film. It showcase what the love of family is all about and acceptance of each parents and kids flaws. It represents culture, diversity and unity.

Where The Crawdads Sing 2022

Based on a novel with the same name by Olivia Newman. Best movie for me, because of it's unique story telling about the girl who live independently but still seeking for love and kindness to other people. Miracle in Cell No.7

Interstellar 2014

I love sci-fi genre movie, and this movie is one of the best for me. I am so amazed as how the story connected from the past, present and the future.

Into the Wild 2007

A sad and devastated film for me, but yet one of my favorite movie. It gives you important lesson to never trust your own instinct. You cannot do things alone, you really need someone who will listen and advice you in life.

Pandora 2016

It's a disaster film that I just recently watched in netflix. It gives a lesson to choose your own battle not everyone can be saved. But at the same time it teaches you on how to be heroic and how to care to the people and to your family in times of crisis.

Train to Busan 2016

I am also a fan of zombie-(post/pre)apocalyptic movies and the first time I watched this film, I feel in love with how the story written well.
